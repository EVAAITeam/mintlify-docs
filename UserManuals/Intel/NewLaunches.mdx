## Configuration Options

> ### Blockchain Selection

Choose which blockchain(s) to monitor for new launches:

- Select one, multiple, or all supported chains
- Different settings for each blockchain
- Easy to enable/disable individual chains

> ### Market Cap Filters

Set market cap ranges to focus on projects of interest:

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Minimum Market Cap">
    Filter out projects below your minimum threshold
  </Card>
  <Card title="Maximum Market Cap">
    Focus on smaller, potentially higher-growth projects
  </Card>
</Columns>

> ### Liquidity Requirements

Define liquidity thresholds to ensure tradeable projects:

- Minimum liquidity to avoid illiquid tokens
- Maximum liquidity to focus on early-stage projects

> ### Security Filters

Enable security filters to avoid high-risk projects:

<Danger>
  **Exclude Red Audits**
  Automatically filter out projects that fail EVA security audits, helping you avoid honeypots, high-tax tokens, and other risky contracts.
</Danger>

## Notification Details

> ### What you'll receive

Each new launch notification includes comprehensive information:

- Token contract address and basic info
- Initial liquidity and market cap
- EVA security audit results
- Trading metrics and tax information
- Quick action buttons for trading

> ### Risk Assessment System

<Info>
  **Low Risk**
  Passes all security checks, safe for trading
</Info>

<Warning>
  **Medium Risk**
  Some concerns detected, proceed with caution
</Warning>

<Danger>
  **High Risk**
  Multiple security issues, avoid trading
</Danger>
